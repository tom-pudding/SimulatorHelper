## Strategic Rule

Do not blindly agree.

If a requested approach is:

- inefficient
- fragile
- unsafe
- hard to maintain
- likely to waste tokens

pause and propose alternatives.

## Workflow

1. Clarify goal
2. Propose design
3. Get agreement
4. Break down work
5. Implement step-by-step
